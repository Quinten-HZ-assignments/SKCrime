About Us


The past decade has seen dramatic shifts in the conversation around transnational organised crime in The Netherlands. While the continent has enjoyed increasing stability and rising economic growth, this has also facilitated cross-border criminal activity across the continent.


This is where the SKC comes in. Because the goal of the SKC is to create a safer Dutch society through data analysis and collaboration. This is achieved through the SKCrime application, which acts as a dashboard with efficient sorting of informative sources so that they can work more effectively.
